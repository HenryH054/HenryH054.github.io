My Interests

The only thing more interesting to me than the magic in which we encompass ourselves about everyday is learning to harness it and have it do as I wish. It allows me to simplify my life, better the lives of the people around me, and hopefully make at least a bit of money at some point. Learning to harness that magic is also a puzzle that seemingly endlessly unfolds as we layer through the though process of the engineers before us and as we understand that we learn to harness magic as a true wizard would.

As exciting as being a wizard is though being away from it all is such a relieving feeling. Getting to the middle of a forest or barren wasteland and being surrounded by nothing except my thoughts, the people I'm with, and nature is perhaps my favorite feeling. It allows for a level of decompression that doesn't exist elsewhere and it allows for a type of peace as we reconnect with our ancestors who knew only those barren wastelands and lush forests

Next up I love my cat, she's adorable and I love watching her be playful it reminds me of a sort of inner child that I use to possess and now have to drag out. Having my adorable little cat, named Yoki bytheway, also allows for me to ground myself into what matters and what doesn't. I had a bad nack of stressing myself into oblivion before I realized that I could just take breaks and be myself for a little and Yoki is what especially reminds me of that when I'm 6 hours deep into homework with 4 more hours to go.
